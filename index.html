Designed for people with low vision

Point your camera at text. Hear it read aloud.

Live Read helps people with vision impairments read printed labels, menus, mail, and more. Instantly, with clear spoken feedback.

Download on the App Store Contact us

How it works

  • Open Live Read and point the camera at the text.
  • Tap once to start or stop reading.
  • Adjust speaking rate and voice in system settings.

Why Live Read

  • Simple interaction. A single tap controls reading so it stays easy to use.
  • Works with accessibility. VoiceOver labels, Dynamic Type, high contrast, and reduced motion.
  • Privacy first. Text recognition runs on your device. No account required.

Accessibility Statement

Effective Date: October 2025

Live Read is designed for people with vision loss. The app is built to work with Apple accessibility features and to meet the intent of WCAG 2.2 accessibility guidance.

Our Commitment

Live Read works naturally with accessibility features to make reading clear, consistent, and easy to control.

  • VoiceOver: All controls are labeled and read aloud with clear, concise descriptions.
  • Dynamic Type: Text automatically scales to match system font size preferences.
  • Contrast and Clarity: Interface colors and typography follow Apple’s Human Interface Guidelines for legibility.
  • Reduce Motion: The app honors system settings to limit or remove motion effects.
  • Simple Interaction: Reading is controlled with a single tap, making the app simple to use.

Feedback

If you experience any accessibility barriers, please contact us.

Accessibility Statement

Effective Date: October 2025

Live Read is designed for people with vision loss. It works with Apple accessibility features and follows WCAG 2.2 guidelines for accessibility.

Our Commitment

  • VoiceOver: All controls are labeled and read aloud clearly.
  • Dynamic Type: Text scales with system font preferences.
  • Contrast & Clarity: Colors and typography follow Apple’s Human Interface Guidelines.
  • Reduce Motion: Motion effects respect system settings.
  • Simple Interaction: Reading is controlled with one tap for ease of use.

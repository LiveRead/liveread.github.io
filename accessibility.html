Accessibility Statement

Effective date: November 2025

Live Read is designed for people with vision loss. It works with Apple accessibility features and follows WCAG 2.2 guidance.

Our commitments

  • VoiceOver. All controls are labeled and announced in a predictable order.
  • Dynamic Type. Text scales with your system font size.
  • Contrast & clarity. High-contrast colors and readable typography.
  • Reduce Motion. Animations respect system settings.
  • Simple interaction. One tap controls reading; no menus or accounts.
  • Privacy. Text recognition runs on-device; content is not uploaded.

Known limitations

  • Very small or low-contrast print may be harder to recognize.
  • Some third-party voices must be downloaded in iOS Settings before use.

Feedback

If you encounter an accessibility barrier, we want to fix it.

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 2025

Live Read respects your privacy. We collect no personal data unless you choose to contact us or provide feedback.

Information We Collect

  • No account, login, or tracking is required to use the app.
  • Device permissions (camera, speech) are used only to read and speak text aloud.
  • When you email us, your address and message are stored solely for support responses.

How We Use Information

We use any information you provide only to respond to support requests, improve app reliability, and address accessibility issues.

Third-Party Services

Live Read does not use analytics, ads, or third-party tracking. The app relies only on Apple frameworks that respect user privacy.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, please contact us.

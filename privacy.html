Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-10-19

Live Read is designed to assist people with vision impairments by reading visible text aloud. We prioritize privacy:

  • No account required.
  • No cloud OCR by default. Text is processed on-device when possible.
  • No analytics cookies on this website.

Information We Collect

We do not collect images or recognized text. If you email us at livereadapp@gmail.com, we receive your email and message.

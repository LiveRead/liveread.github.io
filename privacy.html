Live Read Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 2025

Our Promise

Live Read was built for privacy first. We do not collect, store, or share personal information. Everything the app does, including camera use, text recognition, and speech, is handled entirely on your iPhone.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, location, or usage data.
  • We do not track you, show ads, or use analytics services.
  • We do not upload photos, video, or recognized text to any server.

All processing happens locally using Apple on-device frameworks.

How Camera Access Works

Live Read needs your camera to see printed text and read it aloud. The camera view is never recorded or saved. You can disable camera access anytime in iOS Settings under Live Read.

Policy Updates

If we ever change how Live Read handles data, we will update this page and post a new effective date.

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 2025

Our Promise

Live Read was built for privacy first. We do not collect, store, or share personal information. Everything the app does, including camera use, text recognition, and speech, is handled entirely on your iPhone.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • No personal data. We do not collect your name, email, or location.
  • No analytics or ads. We do not track usage or show advertisements.
  • No uploads. Photos, video, and recognized text never leave your device.

All processing happens locally using Apple’s on-device frameworks.

Camera Access

Live Read requires the camera to read printed text aloud. The camera feed is never recorded, stored, or transmitted. You can disable camera access anytime under iOS Settings → Live Read.

Policy Changes

If this policy changes, we’ll update this page and include a new effective date.
